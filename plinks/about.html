Welcome to BIG-5 Safari

We offer the world's most exciting adventures. Our mission is to provide exceptional experiences that inspire travelers to fall in love with Africa. We are dedicated to wildlife conservation and community development, ensuring that our journeys make a positive impact on the planet.

Our story

Founded by Jessica and Michael, who met while working as wildlife guides in Botswana

2012

Launched a small collection of hand-crafted safari itineraries in Southern Africa

2013

Expanded to East Africa and introduced a range of group tours, private journeys, and family adventures

2016

Opened an office in New York and partnered with luxury travel agency Verve to provide seamless, personalized service

2020
